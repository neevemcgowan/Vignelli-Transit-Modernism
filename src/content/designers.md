# Key Designers of Transit Modernism

### **Massimo Vignelli**
- Lead designer of the NYC Subway Map. (1972)
- Known for strict grids, clarity, Helvetica, and systematic design.
- Applied modernism to civic environments.

### **Otl Aicher**
- Designed Munich Olympics pictograms (1972).
- Created the modern blueprint for universal signage.
- Inspired worldwide transportation iconography.

### **Lance Wyman**
- Mexico City Metro identity system (1968).
- Used symbols to communicate across languages.

### **Jock Kinneir & Margaret Calvert**
- Designed UK Road Sign System (1957-1967).
- Established rules still used today: hierarchy, type size, clarity, contrast.
